Congressional Foster Youth"Shadow Day 2014"

On May 29, 2014, the Congressional Caucus on Foster Youth held the third annual "Congressional Foster Youth Shadow Day." I was one of nearly 70 current and former foster youth from across the country that gathered on Capitol Hill to shadow Members of Congress and advocate for changes to the child welfare system. What an amazing opportunity to spend time with one of the most senior members of Congress and experience what goes into his day as he strives to make a better world for not only the members of our district, but all American citizens.

 

Words of Encouragement Speaking from experience.

Last June, I was invited to speak for the graduation celebration of an alternative school. Many of the students deal with mental disorders and have overcome so much, so this celebration was one of high order in my heart. Having had similar experiences as many of the students, I was humbled to share words of encouragement, but most importantly, to cheer on the graduates along with their fellow schoolmates, teachers, family, and friends.

Black Leaders and Achievers Caucus BLAC Summit Detroit.

I participated in the Detroit Cohort of Teach for America's BLAC Summit. Not only did I grow as an individual, I met amazing people and became involved with TFA as National Partnership Coordinator on the Strategic Initiatives and Partnerships team!
The Black Leaders & Achievers Caucus (BLAC) is a leadership development program for undergraduate students who identify as Black/African American and/or from a low income background. Students participating in BLAC will exposed to activities, workshops and resources to allow them to uncover their identity, explore and build character, build authentic leadership and career readiness skills. At the completion of BLAC, all participants will be considered BLAC Scholars who will form a continuous campus learning team that is armed with the necessary tools to be campus and community leaders.  The mission of BLAC is to arm students of color to lead with passion and purpose while unveiling their true story and authentic voice.
